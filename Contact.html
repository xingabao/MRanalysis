Start Development

2023.07.07

We are excited to announce the commencement of development for our Mendelian Randomization website. This online platform aims to provide researchers and scientists with a powerful tool for exploring causal relationships in genetic epidemiology. As we begin this journey, our focus is on creating a user-friendly interface that will make complex Mendelian Randomization analyses more accessible to the scientific community.
